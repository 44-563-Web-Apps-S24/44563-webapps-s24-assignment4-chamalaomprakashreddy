IRINA.

They've gone....

CHEBUTIKIN.

And they forgot to say good-bye to me.

IRINA.

But why is that?

KULIGIN.

We won't reform Ivan Romanovitch! We won't reform him!

CHEBUTIKIN.

If only I was apprenticed to you! Then I'd reform.

IRINA.

Feodor has shaved his moustache! I can't bear to look at him.
